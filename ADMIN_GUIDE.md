# Portfolio Admin Dashboard - User Guide

Welcome to the admin dashboard guide! Your portfolio is equipped with a powerful, custom-built visual editor that lets you manage your site without touching a single line of code.

Here is everything you need to know to get started.

## 1. Logging In
To keep the site clean, the admin login button is completely hidden from public view.
- Move your mouse to the **bottom-left corner** of the screen.
- Click the invisible square (around 10 pixels from the corner).
- A login prompt will appear. Enter your password (the default is `admin123`).

## 2. The Admin Panel
Once logged in, a control panel will appear in the bottom right.
- **Enable Visual Editor**: This is the master switch. Click this to unlock text editing, style inspection, and dynamic additions.
- **Mobile Toggle**: Check this box to instantly preview how your site looks on a smartphone.
- **Global Colors**: Pick new colors for your Background, Surface (cards), Primary (buttons/accents), and Accent (highlights).
- **No Pic Border**: Checkboxes to independently hide the golden/colored border around your profile picture in Light or Dark mode.
- **Update Password**: Change your admin password for security.
- **Save All Changes**: **Crucial!** Always click this when you are done making edits.

## 3. Editing Text
With the **Visual Editor** enabled:
1. Click on almost any text on the page (your name, bio, job titles, descriptions).
2. A cursor will appear. Simply type to make your changes.
3. For links and buttons, hover over them and a small edit icon will appear—click it to change where the button links to.
4. When finished, click **Save All Changes** in the main admin panel.

## 4. The Universal Style Inspector
Want to change the look of a specific card, button, or paragraph? 
1. Make sure the **Visual Editor** is enabled.
2. Move your mouse around the page. You will see a dashed blue box highlighting elements.
3. **Click** on any highlighted element to freeze it.
4. A floating **Style Inspector** will appear. 
5. Use this inspector to customize the Background color, Text color, Font Family, Font Size, and Font Weight specifically for that element.
6. Click **Save Style** inside the inspector panel to finalize it.

## 5. Adding & Removing Content
Throughout the page, you'll see green buttons (e.g., `+ Add Subject`, `+ Add Experience`, `+ Add Custom Section`).
- **To Add**: Click the button, fill out the form in the popup modal, and hit submit. The new content is added to the page and saved to the database automatically.
- **To Delete**: While the Visual Editor is enabled, hover over any dynamic card (like a skill or experience block). A small `×` or `Delete` button will appear in the top right corner. Click it to permanently remove the item.

## 6. Managing Images
- **Main Portrait**: Hover over your profile picture in the top section and click the pencil icon to upload a new headshot.
- **Gallery**: Scroll down to the gallery section and click `+ Add Photo` to upload new images. You can also reorder them using the arrows.

## 7. Saving Your Work
- **Automatic Saves**: Adding/Deleting cards, adding FAQs, and uploading images are saved automatically the moment you do them.
- **Manual Saves**: Editing text paragraphs, changing Global Colors, or modifying layouts **require** you to click the **Save All Changes** button in the main admin panel. If you click "Exit" without saving, those changes will revert.

Enjoy building your perfect portfolio!
